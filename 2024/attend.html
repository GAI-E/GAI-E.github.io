About Shanghai

Welcome to Shanghai, China! Shanghai, a municipality directly under the central government of China, is a modern international metropolis with a spirit of inclusiveness, pursuit of excellence, broad-mindedness, and humility. Located at the estuary of the Yangtze River in East China and facing the Pacific Ocean, Shanghai forms part of the Yangtze River Delta, one of China's most robust, open and innovative regions.

As China's largest economic powerhouse and an important international financial center, Shanghai achieved a GDP of 4.72 trillion yuan in 2023, up 5 percent year-on-year. Shanghai is also a leading international trade and consumption center, with its ports' total throughput the highest in the world. In 2023, Shanghai's total imports and exports amounted to 4.21 trillion yuan, marking a record high, while the city's total retail sales of consumer goods surged by 12.6 percent. Shanghai is an important global shipping center and an open hub, implementing the RCEP (Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership) agreement and accelerating the construction of the Waigaoqiao and Hongqiao national import trade promotion and innovation demonstration zones.
